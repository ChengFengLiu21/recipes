Recipe for omelette

Description

Omelette with Spring Onion Filling is a simple yet flavorful dish that combines the fluffiness of eggs with the fresh, aromatic crunch of spring onions. The eggs are beaten and seasoned lightly, then cooked until set but tender. A generous filling of finely chopped spring onions, sometimes saut√©ed for added sweetness, is added in the middle or folded into the omelette. This dish is versatile and quick, perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even a light meal. The balance of creamy eggs and the mildly sharp taste of spring onions makes it both comforting and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Spring Oninos
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat the pan till very hot with olive oil
  2. Beat your eggs, mix in the salt
  3. Pour thin layer of eggs on pan and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Sprinkle on the spring onions that have been chopped finely
  5. Roll the egg so that it forms a nice shape
  6. Put on plate, add pepper if you want
